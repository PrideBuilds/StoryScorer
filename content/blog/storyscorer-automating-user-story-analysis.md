## The Challenge of Writing Good User Stories

Teams often struggle with:

- **Inconsistent quality** across user stories

- **Lack of expertise** in requirements writing
- **Manual analysis** that's error-prone and slow

## How StoryScorer Solves This

StoryScorer uses advanced AI to automatically analyze your user stories and provide detailed feedback on each INVEST criterion.

### Automated INVEST Analysis

For each user story, StoryScorer evaluates:

1. **Independent**: Checks if the story can stand alone
2. **Negotiable**: Ensures the story allows for implementation flexibility
3. **Valuable**: Verifies clear user or business value
4. **Estimable**: Assesses if the story can be accurately estimated
5. **Small**: Determines if the story is appropriately sized
6. **Testable**: Validates that acceptance criteria enable testing

### Detailed Feedback

StoryScorer provides:

- **Scores** for each INVEST criterion (0-100)
- **Specific feedback** on what's working and what needs improvement
- **Recommendations** for enhancing your stories
- **Overall score** to track story quality
